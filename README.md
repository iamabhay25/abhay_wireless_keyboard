# Abhay's Wireless Keyboard
This is my wireless keyboard for everyday personal use.<br>
Bill of Materials:<br>
<br>
Switches & Keycaps
78 × Cherry MX-style switches<br>
1 × Keycap set (supports your layout)<br>
Must include:<br>
1 × 6.25u spacebar<br>
2 × 2.25u keys<br>
<br>
Stabilizers (PCB Mount – Cherry Style)<br>
1 × 6.25u stabilizer (spacebar)<br>
2 × 2.25u stabilizers<br>
<br>
Electronics<br>
1 × Custom PCB (78-key)<br>
1 × Raspberry Pi Pico<br>
78 × Diodes (1N4148)<br>
1 × USB cable (USB-A → USB-C)<br>
<br>
Hardware <br>
PCB Mount (Tray Mount)<br>
4 × M3 screws (6–8 mm length)<br>
4 × M3 standoffs (~5 mm height)<br>
Case Assembly<br>
4 × M3 screws (10–14 mm length)<br>
<br>
Case Parts<br>
Top case <br>
Bottom case<br>
<br>
Mounting<br>
Header pins for Pico (male headers, 2×20)<br>
<br>
Sound / Feel <br>
Case foam (bottom foam)<br>
PCB foam <br>
<br>
Final Count Summary<br>
Switches: 78<br>
Diodes: 78<br>
Stabilizers: 3<br>
Screws: 8 total (M3)<br>
Standoffs: 4<br>
Microcontroller: 1 Pico<br>
